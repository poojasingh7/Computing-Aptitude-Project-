The Blood Bank Management System is a software application designed to efficiently manage and store information about blood donors, recipients, and available blood units.
It helps hospitals and blood banks maintain accurate records of blood inventory, ensuring the timely availability of required blood types. 
The system simplifies the process of matching donors with recipients based on blood group compatibility, reducing delays during emergencies. 
It also enhances transparency, safety, and reliability in blood collection, storage, and distribution. 
Overall, this system streamlines blood bank operations, improving efficiency and saving lives.
